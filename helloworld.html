My pet blue......

I met and fell in love with my pet fish Mr blue when i saw him swimming in the aquarium in the pet store and immediately my baby nephew named him blue.
Mr Blue usually spends most of time swimming lazily in his bowl during the day and seems to be more active at night time.
Mr blue eats fish flakes, we feed him in the morning and evening. Usually when he is full, he just swims away from the top of his bowl.
